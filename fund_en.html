How to apply for TEF-Net Scholarship

Students can download TEF-Net scholarship application and follow following instructions.

Fill out application

Students can download application directly from www.TEF-Net.com and fill out the application

Application approval by advisor

Students submit their application to advisor. Advisor provides recommendation and approve the applications.

Submits applications

Advisor must check and submit all students’ completed applications directly to TEF-Net within the deadline.

Document check

TEF-Net team will check all applications and send them to scholarship donors for selection. Awardees will be announced at www.TEF-Net.com

Eligibility and Requirements

     1. Applicant must be pre-selected by advisor and the school must participate in TEF-Net.*

     2. Applicant is a student in low income family who is willing to study to the best of his/her ability.

     3. Applicant must be in diligent, disciplined, and moral student.

     *If your school has not participated in TEF-Net, please contact us at TEFNetinfo@gmail.com for more information.


Submission requirements

     1. Completed application with advisor recommendation and approval

     2. Applicant current transcript

Download TEF-Net Scholarship Application

Submission Deadline

     1. TEF-Net scholarship will be opened for submission every year between December 1st - December 15th. Students can download application at
     Download TEF-Net Scholarship Application and submit application to TEFNetinfo@gmail.com

     2. All awardee will be announced every year on January 1st at www.TEF-Net.com

     3. TEF-Net Scholarships will be delivered by TEF-Net members on Thai children day at awardees’ schools.


Scholarship selection processes

Once TEF-Net team receives completed students’ applications, our team will contact scholarship donors and send applications for their consideration and selection. TEF-Net can also participate in scholarship selection processes for all applications if scholarship donors would like us to help in these processes.
