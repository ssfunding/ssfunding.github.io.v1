About TEF-Net

Thailand Educational Funding Network: TEF-Net was established in November 2017 and has begun providing scholarships to children in Thailand. TEF-Net commits to support and improve students educational opportunities by directly providing funds to students, who suffer from inadequate resources related to education.TEF-Net is a non-profit organization, which has been created by a group of graduate students to pursue the goals in helping and supporting Thai students. TEF-Net is dedicated to students in low income family by providing scholarship directly to students. 100% of all donation to our program will be distributed to students. TEF-Net will not charge any administrative cost for our program. All donations are guaranteed to be sent directly to sponsored students.

TEF-Net 4 main goals are

Scholarships

To provide funding for students in low income family and support their educational opportunities

Students’ lunch

To help students who need supports for their lunch at school

Stationary

To provide funding for students who need stationary for educational purposes

Uniform

To support students in low income family by providing new school uniform



Please contact our TEF-Net team at www.TEF-Net.com or TEFNetinfo@gmail.com for more information about our program and how you can help students.
